# Tiny Dictator — Content Guide

## Decision writing formula
Each decision should have:
1. A funny advisor proposal.
2. Two clear choices.
3. Short-term effects.
4. Optional hidden consequence.
5. Optional follow-up event.

## Good example
Advisor: General Boom
Text: "Citizens are walking too slowly. I suggest military marching lessons for everyone."
Choice A: "Absolutely not"
Choice B: "March, citizens, march!"

## Tone
Absurd, punchy, fictional. Avoid real-world political references.
