# Closed Alpha — Feedback Questions

Use these prompts for optional surveys or interviews. Keep answers short.  
Do **not** require a survey after every run.

---

## After 1–3 runs (optional)

1. Did you understand what the four resources (treasury, happiness, order, elite loyalty) meant?
2. Did any choice feel meaningless (both options the same)?
3. Did you notice active laws changing the country?
4. Which advisor do you remember most clearly? Why?
5. Did any joke or situation feel repeated?
6. Did any card feel confusing or unclear?
7. Did a crisis feel different from a normal decision?
8. Did your ending feel earned, random, or confusing?
9. Was the average run too short, about right, or too long?
10. Did medals / palace / ending archive make you want to play again?
11. Anything offensive, culturally unclear, or in poor taste?
12. Any crash, freeze, soft-lock, or save issue?

---

## In-game feedback form fields

Already supported in the alpha build:

- Feedback type
- Optional related decision ID
- Optional related run ID
- Rating (1–5)
- Short free-text comment
- Checkboxes: confusing / repeated / technical bug / offensive / favorite moment

---

## Owner interview tips

- Ask for concrete decision titles or paraphrases when possible.
- Prefer “what confused you” over “did you like it.”
- Separate humor taste from comprehension failures.
- Capture P0 bugs immediately with the issue template.
